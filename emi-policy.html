This Privacy Policy explains how IBM PRO CONSTRUCTION (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information when you use the EMI Calculator mobile application (the “App”). By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described herein.

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone in that age group. If you become aware that a child has provided us personal information, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.
